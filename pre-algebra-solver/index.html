Standard Pre-Algebra Solving Steps
  1. Distribute to eliminate parentheses.
  2. Combine like terms on each side of the equation.
  3. Use addition or subtraction to move all variable terms to one side.
  4. Use addition or subtraction to move all constant terms to the opposite side.
  5. Use multiplication or division to isolate the variable.
