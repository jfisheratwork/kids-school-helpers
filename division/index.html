How to Solve: Multi-Digit Division
  1. Multiply the bottom ones digit by each digit in the top number, right to left. Write the carry above the next digit if the product is 10 or more.
  2. Add a zero placeholder for the next row.
  3. Multiply the bottom tens digit (and so on) by the top number.
  4. Sum the partial products.
  5. If decimals are present, count the decimal places in the factors and place the decimal point in the final product.
